Executive Assistant Briefing™

Executive Assistant Briefing™ is a product concept designed to help leaders stay ahead of missed actions, anomalies, and fragmented communication across email and WhatsApp. The product is currently in development, with a beta target for Q3 2026.

Product direction Turn executive communications into clear briefings, alerts, and actionable follow-up.
Target user Managers and executives who need signal clarity without technical friction.
Stack direction n8n, WhatsApp API, IMAP, Supabase, and LLM orchestration.

What the product does

  • Analyzes incoming communications to surface anomalies, missed tasks, and emerging priorities.
  • Builds structured executive briefings instead of leaving leaders buried in message volume.
  • Supports fast interaction through WhatsApp for users who need immediate operational visibility.

Why it matters

  • Many leaders operate in information-heavy environments where the real problem is not access, but interpretation.
  • Important actions are often missed because signal is mixed with low-value noise.
  • The product is designed to reduce that burden while preserving user trust and data sovereignty.

Design principles

  • Operational usefulness over novelty.
  • Privacy-aware architecture and minimal data retention.
  • Direct language for decision-makers rather than technical jargon.

Current status

The product is in active concept and build preparation. This portfolio page exists to document the product direction, the operational problem it addresses, and the system thinking behind it before public beta.
