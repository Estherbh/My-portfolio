How the work is delivered

The portfolio is meant to feel operational rather than performative. Each artifact is presented in the format most natural to the work itself.

Sheets where reporting logic matters

Google Sheets is used when the goal is to expose formulas, KPI structure, or source-to-output transparency.

Looker Studio where stakeholder visibility matters

Looker Studio is used when the priority is quick interpretation and executive-level review.

HTML where explanation and narrative matter

HTML case studies and one-pagers are used when the portfolio needs to show communication clarity, not just tool knowledge.

Sanitized pages where confidentiality matters

Private dashboard work is documented at a responsible level that shows real capability without exposing protected systems or data.
