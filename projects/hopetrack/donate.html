Why this flow is structured this way

The form keeps the interface calm and direct while still capturing the decision points that matter most in campaign analytics.

  • Preset amount tracking for clean event parameters
  • Monthly vs one-time donor choice for lifecycle analysis
  • Email domain extraction for non-sensitive conversion review
  • Friction-light thank-you state with no backend dependency
