Education for All - DRC Congo 🇨🇩

Building access where classrooms need infrastructure, supplies, and consistent teaching support.

Education for All in DRC Congo funds school supplies, teacher training, and local classroom upgrades for rural communities. The goal is to remove the practical barriers that keep children out of school.

Girls in School Initiative - Bangladesh 🇧🇩

Reducing dropout risk through scholarships, mentoring, and support for the families behind each learner.

This initiative focuses on keeping girls in school by addressing the financial and social pressures that lead to dropout. Scholarships and mentoring are paired with family engagement to sustain attendance.
