Mission, leadership, and operating model

Who We Are

HopeTrack International is a fictional organization built to demonstrate what a disciplined, donor-ready education campaign can look like when design, storytelling, and measurement work together.

Built around measurable education outcomes.

HopeTrack is designed around practical interventions: classroom support, teacher readiness, scholarship continuity, and youth skills development. Every page in the experience is written to feel public-facing while still supporting a serious analytics story underneath.

For this portfolio project, the goal is not just to “look nonprofit.” It is to create a website that shows operational clarity, credible messaging, and the ability to connect user experience with campaign measurement.

“We believe fundraising should be as transparent, intentional, and accountable as the programs it claims to support.”

Impact timeline

A grounded timeline makes the organization feel real and gives the campaign narrative a sense of operating history.

2020

Founded in Geneva

The organization launched with a focus on education access, transparency, and practical field support.

2021

First program in Kenya

Digital access and youth readiness became the first live initiative in the portfolio.

2022

Expanded to DRC Congo

Infrastructure support and teacher training extended the operating footprint into rural communities.

2023

5,000 children reached

A major milestone that pushed the organization toward more structured reporting and campaign planning.

2024

Bangladesh initiative launched

The girls' education program opened with scholarship and mentoring support at its core.

2025

$200,000 raised

Fundraising maturity created the case for a more sophisticated digital campaign stack.

Values that shape execution

Accountability

Impact
