Unified Marketing Reporting Template

This project demonstrates how multi-source marketing and donor data can be consolidated in Google Sheets before full BI integration, then surfaced through a stakeholder-facing Looker Studio layer.

Open Google Sheet Open Looker Studio Back to portfolio

What it includes

  • Source tabs for social, web, email, and CRM-style data.
  • A KPI dashboard structured across the marketing funnel.
  • Weekly and monthly reporting views for non-technical teams.
  • Data quality checks, governance notes, and Looker-ready tabs.

Why it matters

  • Shows Google Sheets as an interim analytics operating layer.
  • Demonstrates KPI structure, not just dashboard decoration.
  • Bridges raw reporting work and executive-level visualization.
  • Fits roles where teams need useful reporting before formal BI maturity.
