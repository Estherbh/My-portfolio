Problem

  • Operational signals were spread across separate sources.
  • Reporting cycles were slower than stakeholders needed.
  • Decision-makers lacked a unified monitoring interface.

Contribution

  • Structured dashboard views for faster decision support.
  • Improved reporting flow across multiple categories of information.
  • Helped translate sensitive operational data into clearer interfaces.

What it demonstrates

  • Full-stack dashboard thinking in a non-public environment.
  • KPI structuring under confidentiality and risk constraints.
  • Responsible handling of protected systems and data.
